Net.Art Assignment

This was a collaborative assignment with Enoch. We created this website as a response to the net.art piece Life Sharing by Eva and Franco Mattes. This website was inspired by the project, in which the artists published their entire hard drive to the internet for all to see, so we made our website look like a stylized version of a typical Windows 98 desktop at the time that this piece was published. We try to make you feel as if you are in a computer when reading about a piece about being in a computer. Deep.

Assignment 4
