Super Smash Bros. Melee for the Nintendo Gamecube is a 20 year old party game that has maintained a strong competitive scene from its launch to this very day. I personally have always loved this game for the memories its given me of playing in tournaments or on a couch with my friends. As such, I wanted to make a tribute to this game and its incredibly robust competitive scene. I found it very interesting to do the research for this project, as it felt like a trip down memory lane.
