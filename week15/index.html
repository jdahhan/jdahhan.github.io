Peach is one of the most unique top tier characters in melee. Her slower floaty characteristics make it harder for her to play aggressively, but make her more suited for a bait and punish playstyle. The undisputed master of this is Swedish former pro Armada, who spend several years as the #1 player in the world before retiring.
